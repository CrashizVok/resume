About Me

My name is Regő Ronyecz, also known as CrashiZ — I am a 17-year-old high school student that's really invested in cybersecurity.

I began my journey on TryHackMe but now work on Hack The Box mostly, I'm hoping to obtain my Penetration Tester and Defensive Security Analyst certificates as soon as I can.

I compete in CTF competitions regularly to practice real world skills, while also testing my limits. I am hoping that someday I will be a part of a great Penetration Testing team.

I primarily use Burp Suite, Nmap and Metasploit; however, I have used many other tools to get to where I am today; and I do all of my work through a Kali Linux environment.

I will soon attend university to gain my Cybersecurity Engineering degree, and have already committed a large amount of time studying/exercising/developing and honing my skills.
